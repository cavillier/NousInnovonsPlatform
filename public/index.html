Benefits of participating


One main barrier for process innovation into SMEs is the lack time to look for optimisations and test solutions.
However, with the rise of all those trendy applications based on the cloud, potential of improvements is real.
Through this research, we hope to create an environment where students could help organizations to accelerate their pace by improving their awareness.
We are not setting up or rolling out solutions. We only provide you support into the research of waste, the creation of ideas, and the test of solutions.

How are our projects going?
We seek, show, train, and seek again!

The main goal of this platform is fostering reflexion and creating awareness about opportunities offering by new technology.
See below the 12 weeks phases that our students will follow to work on your idea/problem :


  1. You fill this form to submit a project.

  2. Your project starts in Mars or October with a student. You schedule a first video call.

  3. You meet the student and he will ask you more details about your project.

  4. The student looks for solutions, tests them and picks two. He makes one presentation video for each solution.

  5. You watch the video and schedule a second video call.

  6. During this second meeting, you will redefine your needs and tests yourself the solution.

  7. The student continues his reasearch, either by looking for another solution, or by going deeper in the one selected.


  1. A new video is made with a stronger focus on how to use the solution everyday. A third video call is planned.

  2. This time, the student and you will focus on the impact of this solution on your daily processes. You will improve the design of the solution.

  3. The student will realise a last video focusing on how to integrate this solution into your company.

  4. A last time, you evaluate the video and schedule a video call.

  5. For this last online meeting, you will try to point out all the barriers and risks to adopt this solution.

  6. To conclude this project, the student writes a report about your solution, its impacts and risks.
